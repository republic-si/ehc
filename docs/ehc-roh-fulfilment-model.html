Zero ROH working capital pre-pick

Bottles sit on EHC's shelf as maker-owned stock. ROH holds nothing, buys nothing, until a subscription box is picked.

Curation pool scales for free

Every maker on the EHC shelf is a curation option. Adding a maker to the pool costs ROH nothing, unlike direct pre-buys.

COGS event = pick, not ship-in

The moment EHC pulls a bottle for a ROH box is the moment cash leaves ROH. Cost timing matches revenue timing.

Today

ROH pre-buys from each maker to hold subscription stock. Cash out at ship-in, sits on shelves for weeks. Adding a maker means a fresh purchase order.

Under EHC

Makers consign to Wildau. ROH picks from the shared shelf per box. Cash leaves ROH only at pick. Adding a maker is a shelf entry, not a purchase.
